About the conference

We aim to expose young innovators to new tech

We are a team of high-school students working to curate a conference by youth, for youth.

We will feature multiple successful teenage speakers, who will be talking about their journeys, and who will provide advice to you so you can conduct research and work on your own projects within STEM.

This is a day full of panels, presentations, and a workshop to help you learn and get further in STEM. We currently have 12 speakers, and 8 sessions, with breaks to ensure ocular rest.

You can join for part of or all of it. We know some people have short attention spans (we do too :)), so come for the sessions you'd like to be there for. Sessions and agenda are below.
