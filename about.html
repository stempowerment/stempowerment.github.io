About Us

Welcome to STEMpowerment Innovation!

Around the world, there are millions of kids who are disconnected from STEM. STEMpowerment is a youth-led initiative that aims to empower these youth to dive into STEM, and give them the tools and knowledge to start their journey into STEM fields. We aim to spark and ignite the passion in youth around the world for STEM, creating the next generation of Scientists, Mathematicians, Engineers, and so much more.

What We Offer

Conference

We host conferences aimed to empower youth around the world to pursue STEM fields, with leading experts as speakers.

Workshops/Events

Through workshops and events, youth around the world are empowered to pursue STEM fields, giving them enough knowledge to start their journey into STEM.

Contests

We regularly host contests focused on pushing youth to enter STEM, such as writing contests, video contests, and so much more.

Resources

To empower youth to get into STEM, we regularly organize a list of resources that help youth get the tools they need to get into STEM.
